Ryan Vella's Webpage

About

I am a PhD student at the Max Planck Institute for Chemistry under the supervison of Prof. Jos Lelieveld. I work in the group of Environmental Modelling in the Climate System led by Prof. Holger Tost at the Institute for Atmospheric Physics, Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz. I earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Physics & Mathematics, followed by a Master of Science degree in Geosciences, both from the University of Malta. Prior to beginning my PhD in Mainz, I completed a fellowship in Earth Observation at the European Space Agency (ESA) in Frascati, Italy.

Research

My main research interests include:

  • Earth System Modelling
  • Numerical models for vegetation dynamics and atmopsheric chemistry
  • Land-biosphere-atmosphere interactions and feedbacks
  • Biogenic volatile organic compounds
  • Secondary organic aerosols

Other research interests:

  • Earth Observation
  • Satellite remote sensing
  • Shipping emissions
  • Light pollution

The Rest
