About

I am a PhD student at the Max Planck Institute for Chemistry under the supervison of Prof. Jos Lelieveld. I work in the group of Environmental Modelling in the Climate System led by Prof. Holger Tost at the the Institute for Atmospheric Physics, Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz. I earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Physics & Mathematics, followed by a Master of Science degree in Geosciences, both from the University of Malta. Prior to beginning my PhD in Mainz, I completed a fellowship in Earth Observation at the European Space Agency (ESA) in Frascati, Italy.
